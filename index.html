How to Play

To play this exciting game and win up to €1 million, read the following mechanics:

How to win?

  • Answer correctly all 15 questions to win the €1 million.
  • Each question also corresponds to an amount of money.

When is game over?

  • When wrong answer is selected, or
  • When time runs out, or
  • When all 15 questions are correctly.

How are the questions presented?

  • Each question has 4 answer choices, but only 1 is correct.
  • You have 30 seconds for each question.

Life Lines to Help You

  • You have 3 lifelines.
  • 1st icon removes 1 wrong answer
  • 2nd icon removes 2 wrong answers
  • 3rd icon adds 30 sec to the timer
  • Each lifeline can only be used ONCE in the course of the game.
  • You can use any and as many lifelines you want in a single question.
